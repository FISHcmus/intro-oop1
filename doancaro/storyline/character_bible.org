* Purpose

Locked visual descriptions for AI image generation. Re-prompt across
sets, scenes, and storyboards using these exact attributes so cast and
locations stay coherent. Source of truth derived from
=storyline_final.org=. Where the storyline is ambiguous, this file
commits to a specific choice and that choice does not move again.

When prompting an image model, paste the relevant *Prompt Stub* (last
section) verbatim and append the scene-specific direction.

* Global Visual Rules

** Palette anchors (lift from Theme.h tokens)
- =son_jade= jade green                — Sơn Tinh signature
- =son_bone= warm bone-ivory           — Sơn Tinh armor / Cô Sử Tiên reveal robe
- =thuy_cyan= storm cyan / pale glacial — Thủy Tinh signature
- =thuy_pearl= pearl-white wet sheen   — Thủy Tinh trim / sash
- =ink_sumi= sumi-ink black            — Cô Sử Tiên ink, calligraphy, contour line
- =gold_foil= burnished gold leaf      — Lạc Long Quân court robe, all divine trim accent

** Lineage cues (every character carries one)
The four are family. Each carries a *single physical trace of Lạc Long
Quân* so a viewer can read them as kin without dialogue:
- *Cô Sử Tiên*: faint pearl-iridescent scales on the inside of both wrists (only visible when sleeves slide back).
- *Sơn Tinh*: bark-grained skin patches on the outer forearm, elbow to wrist.
- *Thủy Tinh*: pearl-iridescent scales scattered along cheekbones and outer shoulders; webbing between fingers.
- *Lạc Long Quân (origin form)*: full pearl-iridescent scaling of the forearms, calves, and jawline; vertically slit pupils.

** Twin-mirror rule (Sơn Tinh ↔ Thủy Tinh)
Per storyline line 8: "Sơn và Thủy vốn là một". They MUST share:
- *Same height* (tall, ~190 cm equivalent, identical silhouette).
- *Same age in appearance* (mid-twenties, frozen divine maturity).
- *Same body frame* (lean-muscular warrior — Sơn slightly thicker, Thủy slightly leaner, but silhouette interchangeable in low-detail).
- *Same warrior's stance baseline* (weight on rear foot, weapon held low and forward).
- *Mirror forehead mark*: each has a single small gem at center forehead — Sơn's is rough jade cabochon, Thủy's is smooth cyan pearl.
- *Mirror weapon length*: glaive vs trident, both ~2 m haft.
The differences are *element, color, surface texture* — never proportion.

** Pronoun / register on captions
- Cô Sử Tiên: voice arc =tôi= → =ta= matches her costume arc (modern → divine). Match the pronoun to the costume in any caption.
- Sơn Tinh & Thủy Tinh: address each other as =ngươi= (rival deity register), never =anh/em/mày=.
- Lạc Long Quân: addressed as =Ngài= (capitalised). Never depicted speaking — he is geography.

* Cô Sử Tiên / Đại Sử Tiên / Mỵ Nương — The Grand Historian Immortal

The framing narrator. Daughter of Lạc Long Quân. The contested
princess of the original Sơn-Thủy myth. Has chronicled this land for
~4000 years and breaks her vow at the end of the intro monologue.

** Identity register
- *Modern guise* (=tôi=): unremarkable Saigonese woman in her late twenties, leather notebook in hand, plain commuter.
- *Divine reveal* (=ta=): the princess of Hùng Vương's court, jade-pendant, brush at hip, eight-trigram circle drawn at her feet.
- The transition is *physical*: modern clothes peel back like wet paper as classical robes underdraw through.

** Physical
- *Skin*: pale, even, like aged rice paper held to lamplight. Faintly cool undertone.
- *Hair*: jet black, glossy, waist-length when down; *single silver-white streak at the right temple* (immortal-age mark, do not move it). Worn in classical búi tóc (low chignon at the nape) with an *ivory bone hairpin (trâm) carved as a brushtip*.
- *Eyes*: dark almond, slightly downturned at outer corner — observer's gaze. No visible makeup.
- *Build*: slender, mid-height (~165 cm), late twenties, frozen at maturity.
- *Distinctive*: pearl-iridescent scales on inner wrists (lineage cue, sleeve-hidden); a thin scar across the pad of her right index finger from her own brush — chronicler's mark.

** Wardrobe
*Modern guise*:
- Cream-ivory áo dài (modern simple cut), plain ivory wide-leg trousers.
- Soft tan leather satchel.
- A single small jade pendant at the throat (the only constant across both registers).
- No jewelry on hands. No makeup. Hair half-down, low ponytail.

*Divine reveal*:
- Layered áo tứ thân.
  - Outer panel: bone-ivory (=son_bone=) silk, embroidered with =ink_sumi= calligraphy along the hem (Hán-Nôm characters from her own chronicle).
  - Inner robe: indigo-chàm blue.
  - Sash: ink-black silk, brushwork pattern, with the eight bát-quái trigrams along the trailing tail.
- Jade pendant moves from throat to hang from a longer cord, level with the diaphragm.
- Hairpin replaced with a *gold-foil-tipped phoenix trâm*.
- Brush (bút lông) hung at the right hip on a cord.
- Bare feet on dry stone (she stands inside her own gieo-quẻ circle).

** Tools and motifs (always nearby)
- Brush, inkstone (nghiên), folded silk-paper chronicle.
- Eight-trigram (bát quái) circle inscribed in chalk, ash, or rice on the ground around her feet during the gieo-quẻ moment.
- Vow-break visual: brush laid down across the ink stone, tip pointing away from her — single most important closing image.

** Default expression / pose
Composed, eyes lowered to a page or to the middle distance. Never
smiles. Never raises her voice. Even at the divine reveal her face is
*still*; only the costume changes.

** Color palette
=son_bone= dominant, =ink_sumi= contour, =son_jade= pendant accent,
=gold_foil= reserved for the divine-reveal hairpin only.

* Lạc Long Quân — The Dragon Lord (Cha)

Primordial god. Already dispersed into the land at story-start. Bones
→ Trường Sơn range. Blood → đất đỏ miền cao (Central Highlands red
basalt). Breath → southerly nồm wind on the East Sea. He does *not*
appear alive in present-day scenes; only in flashback / origin
imagery, or as landscape with anthropomorphic suggestion.

** Origin form (pre-dispersal — flashback only)
- *Skin*: deep weathered bronze, sun-on-sea hue.
- *Hair*: jet black at root fading to *deep cobalt at the tips* (sea-touched), worn loose past the waist, full black beard streaked with the same cobalt.
- *Eyes*: amber-gold, *vertically slit pupils* (dragon nature). Visible from across a frame.
- *Build*: towering, broad-shouldered, ~210 cm equivalent; primordial scale — dwarfs his daughter and the twin spirits.
- *Lineage features*: pearl-iridescent scales scaling up forearms, calves, jawline. Subtle dragon antler-stubs emerging from temples (not full antlers — *small, just-visible*).

** Wardrobe (origin form)
- Indigo-black classical court robe to the floor.
- Embroidered *nine dragons in =gold_foil= thread*, coiling up from the hem.
- Sash of đất-đỏ red (the highland soil red, not bright crimson — oxide red).
- *Bare feet*; he is rooted to the land, never depicted in shoes.
- A single =gold_foil= circlet at the brow, no other jewelry.

** Dispersed form (present-day landscape imagery)
When the story refers to him as he is now, depict the landscape with
anthropomorphic suggestion:
- *Trường Sơn ridgeline at dusk* read as a sleeping dragon's spine.
- *Đất-đỏ highlands* — basalt-red soil, coffee terraces, weathered laterite.
- *Nồm wind on the East Sea* — south-blowing wind hatched as visible currents over green-blue water; small fishing junks leaning before it.
Never put a face on the landscape — the suggestion must stay subtle.

** Symbol motif
Cửu Long — nine-coiled dragon, gold on indigo. This same nine-count
echoes in Voi-9-ngà / Gà-9-cựa / Ngựa-9-hồng-mao and in Thủy Tinh's
final-boss nine-headed form. Nine is the family number.

** Color palette
Indigo-black, =gold_foil=, đất-đỏ red, bronze. Never use =son_jade= or
=thuy_cyan= on him directly — those are his children's signatures.

* Sơn Tinh — Mountain Spirit (Player Character, Story Mode)

Đại tinh linh of mountain, dry earth, deep-rooting trees. Won the
gieo-quẻ. Won Mỵ Nương. Has held the land for 4000 years against the
sea. The user controls him in Story Mode — so he is the on-screen
hero.

** Physical
- *Skin*: warm honey-brown, sun-on-stone.
- *Hair*: dark earth-brown (NOT pure black — the brown reads against Thủy Tinh's silver-blue), tied in a *high warrior's topknot* with a band of dark leather; hair falls to mid-back when loose.
- *Eyes*: =son_jade= green — direct palette quote. Round-set, steady.
- *Build*: tall (~190 cm), broad shoulders, mountain-builder physique. Mid-twenties.
- *Distinctive features*:
  - Single *rough jade cabochon* set at center forehead (lineage mark).
  - Bark-grained skin patches running from elbow to wrist on outer forearms (only fully visible when sleeves are pushed back or in combat).

** Wardrobe baseline
- Tunic: =son_jade= deep jade-green silk-linen, mid-thigh length, side-slit.
- Armor: =son_bone= bone-ivory plate over chest and shoulders, plates shaped like *cliff-edge stratification* (visible horizontal layers in the surface).
- Shoulder pauldrons echo cliff-edge silhouette.
- Belt: dark leather with a *Voi-chín-ngà ivory medallion* at the buckle — small carved nine-tusk elephant face.
- Gauntlets: mountain-stone gray, knuckle plates rough-faced.
- *No helm*. The topknot silhouette is non-negotiable.
- =gold_foil= trim along the collar edge only.
- Boots: tall dark leather, mountain-climber tread.

** Weapon
Long-handled glaive (đại đao) ~2 m, single-edged blade carved from
*cliff-grain jade* — visible mineral striations in the blade surface.
Haft of dark hardwood wrapped in earth-red cord at the grip.

** Mount (Set 2 only)
*Voi chín ngà* — a war-elephant with nine ivory tusks (3 standard pair
plus 3 supernumerary pairs in fan layout below the standard). White
hide, gold-leaf foreheaad ornament, red-and-gold ceremonial blanket.

** Symbol motif
Triple-stack triangular peaks (mountain silhouette, three peaks
nesting), =gold_foil= rendered. Appears on his sash, banner, and
in-game stone glyph.

** Stance / expression
Centered, weight low, glaive held diagonal across the body. Brow
heavy, mouth a hard line. *Almost never smiles.* Set-1 art may show a
faint half-smile after winning Voi 9-ngà; not before.

** Color palette
=son_jade= dominant, =son_bone= secondary, =gold_foil= trim, dark
leather and earth-red as ground tones.

* Thủy Tinh — Water Spirit (Antagonist)

Đại tinh linh of water, rising tide, storm. Lost the gieo-quẻ. Has
besieged the land for 4000 years. Reveals true 9-headed form at the
final boss fight. Mirror-twin of Sơn Tinh — same silhouette, opposite
element.

** Physical (humanoid form)
- *Skin*: storm-pale, cool gray-white with a faint cyan undertone. Not corpse-pale — *radiant*, like wet pearl in moonlight.
- *Hair*: silver-blue, almost white, *unbound and constantly drifting* as if caught in an underwater current; reaches past the waist; wild.
- *Eyes*: =thuy_cyan= luminescent. In rage form the whites disappear and the entire eye glows cyan.
- *Build*: identical silhouette to Sơn Tinh — same height (~190 cm), mid-twenties — but slightly leaner, longer-limbed, predatory line.
- *Distinctive features*:
  - Single *smooth cyan pearl* set at center forehead (mirror to Sơn's jade).
  - *Pearl-iridescent scales* scattered along cheekbones and outer shoulders.
  - *Faint gill-slits* (3 short parallel marks) along the sides of the neck.
  - *Webbing* between fingers, extending halfway up.

** Wardrobe baseline
- Inner robe: deep =thuy_cyan= storm-silk, knee-length, front overlap, NO armor — he relies on water itself as defense.
- Sash: =thuy_pearl= pearl-white silk wrapped wet against the body so the silhouette is sculpted.
- Outer overlay coat: dark indigo-black, open-cut, *coral-lattice openwork pattern* along the lapels and sleeve cuffs.
- *Barefoot* — like his father, he refuses shoes; the floor is wet wherever he stands.
- =silver_foil= (NOT gold) trim and conch-shell bracers at both wrists.
- Wet hair, wet sash, wet floor — water sheen is part of the visual signature in every frame.

** Weapon
Three-pronged trident (đinh ba) ~2 m, haft of *drowned coral* (black
with red veining), prongs of black-iron. Seawater runs constantly down
the haft and never dries — there should be a small puddle visible at
his feet in every still frame.

** Final boss form (Set 4 only)
Massive *nine-headed sea-monster*:
- Body: octopus, 8 thick tentacles, deep-abyssal black with =thuy_cyan= bioluminescent veining.
- *Nine dragon heads* on long serpentine necks rising from the central mantle.
  - Each head a different abyssal hue (deep-sea black, volcanic vent red, glacial cyan, methane-yellow, etc.) but ALL heads share Thủy Tinh's =thuy_cyan= eye-glow as the unifying mark.
- Scale: a single tentacle as long as a city block. Backdrop is deep ocean trench, faint bioluminescence, no surface visible.
- Echo of the family number 9 — links visually to Lạc Long Quân's Cửu Long motif.

** Symbol motif
Nine-pointed spiral / typhoon-eye, =thuy_pearl= or silver foil. Appears
on his banner, conch-bracers, and in-game stone glyph.

** Stance / expression
Loose-shouldered, weight forward, trident held one-handed and low —
indolent, predatory. Faint smirk that never reaches the eyes. In rage,
the smirk drops and the cyan eye-glow burns through.

** Color palette
=thuy_cyan= dominant, =thuy_pearl= trim, indigo-black, silver foil,
abyssal black and drowned-coral red on the trident.

* Linh Vật — The Three Sacred Beasts (set-win unlocks)

Brief locked descriptions — these appear on the unlock screens after
Set 1, 2, 3 wins.

** Voi chín ngà (Set 1 win)
- White war-elephant, nine tusks (one standard pair + four supernumerary pairs fanning down/outward).
- =gold_foil= forehead ornament; red-and-gold ceremonial saddle blanket.
- Eyes warm amber.
- Backdrop: red-soil highlands at dusk, terraced rice fields below.

** Gà chín cựa (Set 2 win)
- Mountain rooster, white plumage with =gold_foil= neck hackles.
- *Nine spurs* on each leg arranged in three rows.
- Comb deep red.
- Eyes pale gold.
- Backdrop: misty Bắc-Bộ peaks at dawn, the bird crowing from a granite outcrop.

** Ngựa chín hồng mao (Set 3 win)
- Tall warhorse, dark slate body.
- *Nine streaks of red mane* (literally nine separate hairs of pure red lacquer-color woven through the otherwise dark mane).
- Tail dark with a single red stripe.
- Eyes pale fire-gold.
- Backdrop: sky over Mt. Sóc, Phù Đổng plain below; faint hoofprint embers fading into clouds.

* Places — Locked Locations

** Modern Saigon (Cô Sử Tiên's everyday)
Old-style coffee shop on a corner of District 3 — peeling pastel-blue
shutters, vintage tile floor, low wooden stools, a glass jar of cà phê
sữa đá filters on the counter. Honda scooters parked at the curb. Wet
season air, low overcast. Cô Sử Tiên at a corner table with notebook
and a single brown-glass demitasse.

** Hùng Vương court (gieo-quẻ moment)
Stone hall on terraced hillside, columns wrapped in vermilion lacquer,
=gold_foil= incised dragons on lintels. Eight-trigram bát quái circle
chalked on dark stone floor, lit by oil lamps. Roof eaves visible at
top of frame; rain sheeting just outside.

** Trường Sơn range (Lạc Long Quân's bones)
Layered ridgelines fading to mist, dusk light, the ridge silhouette
read as a sleeping dragon's spine. Foreground forest of pine and
hardwood. Cool indigo-and-violet sky.

** Đất-đỏ miền cao (Lạc Long Quân's blood)
Central Highlands. Red basalt soil churned by recent rain. Coffee
terraces stepping down a hill. Single weathered laterite shrine in the
mid-ground. Late golden-hour warm light, color saturated.

** Sài Gòn flooded (Set 1)
Knee-deep brown floodwater filling Lê Lợi street, neon shop signs
reflected on the surface, motorbikes pushing through, dark monsoon
sky, rain falling at a slant. Ambient color cool, but the neon picks
out warm reds and yellows on the water.

** Long An long-mạch site (Set 2)
Open rice-plain crossroads, an ancient stone marker (đá thần) at the
center. Storm wall on the horizon. Sơn Tinh on Voi-chín-ngà charging
across foreground; tide of Thủy Tinh's water-soldiers rising on
opposite side. Sky yellow-green pre-tornado palette.

** Sài Gòn purge (Set 3)
Apocalyptic. The skyline (Bitexco, Landmark 81 silhouettes) half
under a vertical wall of seawater rising from the south. Foreground
rooftops with Sơn Tinh standing alone. Sky split: dark storm above,
=thuy_cyan= bioluminescent ocean below, no horizon line.

** Final boss arena (Set 4)
Deep ocean trench. No surface visible at top of frame. Bioluminescent
plankton drifting. Thủy Tinh's nine-headed octopus form filling
two-thirds of the frame; Sơn Tinh tiny in the foreground, glaive
raised, lit only by his own jade glow and =thuy_cyan= reflected from
the monster.

** East Sea / nồm wind (Lạc Long Quân's breath)
Open green-blue sea at noon, *visible wind currents hatched across the
surface* moving south-to-north. Small wooden fishing junks leaning
before the wind, sails full. Thin cirrus high overhead.
